About Phosphophyllite

A standard, before it was a brand.

We started as a two-person formulation studio with one rule: never ship anything we couldn't explain, ingredient by ingredient. Twelve years later, that rule still decides what goes on the shelf.

Our origin

Why we borrowed a mineral's name.

Phosphophyllite is a real mineral — one of the rarest in the world, found in only a handful of locations. It's prized less for size than for clarity: cut well, it's almost perfectly transparent.

Our founders, both former food scientists, chose the name as a daily test. If an ingredient list, a supply chain, or a packaging claim couldn't hold up to that kind of scrutiny, it didn't ship.

What we hold ourselves to

Four operating principles.

Traceability

Every ingredient linked to a named source, checkable on request.

Simplicity

If a chemist can't explain it in one sentence, it's not in the formula.

Low-impact packaging

Mono-material and refill formats wherever the category allows it.

Independent review

Every formulation cleared by an outside safety panel before launch.
